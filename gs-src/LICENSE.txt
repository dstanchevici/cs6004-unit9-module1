To the student: 
The are all kinds of software licenses and for each type
of license, there are variations of wording. Some are short and sweet,
others are written by legal teams and go on for pages. See
     https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Software_license 
for a description. For an example of a well thought out,
long-ish license, see the Apache license:
   https://www.apache.org/licenses/LICENSE-2.0
